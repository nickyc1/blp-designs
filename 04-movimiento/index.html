The manifesto.

Seven house rules. One movement. We didn't make these up — they came out of two years of open plays, a group chat that wouldn't quit, and a few hard nights when nobody showed.

01
Show up. Hit balls. Stay for the second game.
~ vamos
Open plays · all week
02
Mixed levels welcome. You bring a friend — we'll find you a fourth.
~ todos juegan

03
The crew is the brand.
The kit is the by-product.

Community before commerce
04
Houston is home base. Everywhere else is a road game.
~ pa' Houston

Bring someone who's never played before.

Argue line calls less. Take the next point harder.
