The five directions.

— Click any tile to open
01 Halftime · parade

Halftime — the parade

Bad Bunny PR halftime energy, executed in the brand palette. Music-poster scale type, bilingual flourishes (Vamos a Padel · El Tour · Las Fundadoras), parade pageantry, sun halftones. Founders front and center.

Type  Bebas Neue + Fraunces italic + Caveat Open →
02 Community · clubhouse

Casa — the clubhouse

Community-first. La casa de padel as the metaphor. The crew is the brand — a wall of regulars, founder portraits leading, bilingual touches throughout. Warm, welcoming, photo-led.

Type  Fraunces + Manrope + Caveat Open →
03 Brutalist · drop

Signal v2 — the loud one

Same brutalist poster energy you liked, rebuilt strictly in sage / navy / bone / taupe — the orange is gone. BLP logo lives in the nav. "Dos Latinas · Houston" lives in the ticker. Every drop card uses the brand palette.

Type  Archivo Black + Manrope + JetBrains Mono Open →
04 Tropical · airy

Tropical — an escape

The quiet tropical. Bone backgrounds with sage-gradient horizons, palm-frond SVG silhouettes, and a vintage travel-poster serif. Open plays as itinerary stops, products as packing list, Osaka as your travel companion. Airy, spacious, slow.

Type  Yeseva One + Manrope + Caveat Open →
05 Atelier · slow

Mercado — hecho en Houston

Slow, considered, atelier. Two-Latinas-owned, hand-printed feel. Each product has its own care label with material, GSM, and "Hecho en Houston, TX" stamped on it. Slow shopping. Quiet but warm.

Type  Fraunces + Manrope + JetBrains Mono Open →
For the partnership conversation

Each direction includes a distinct Osaka treatment.

So your friends can see how a partnership renders five different ways: featured artist on the lineup (Halftime), guest at la casa (Casa), collab drop card (Signal), your travel companion (Tropical), and guest curator residency (Mercado). Same Osaka, five visual treatments — a useful preview of how future collabs would render.

How to review
this deck.

01Click each tile  Each direction is a fully working single-page concept — hero, founders moment, the kit, schedule, Osaka section, manifesto, footer. Open them in tabs and flip between.
02Watch the type and the pace  Halftime (Bebas Neue) is loud and parade-paced. Casa (Fraunces + photos) is warm and member-club. Signal (Archivo Black) is hard-edged. Tropical (Yeseva One) is airy and slow. Mercado is quiet and atelier.
03Then read the Osaka section  That's the partnership canary — how would you want a collab to feel? Featured artist? Guest at the casa? Stamped drop? Ally? Guest curator?
04All five stay in palette  Sage, navy, taupe, bone, cream. No invented colors. The BLP logo is used in every direction. Every cut leans on Latina-founders / Houston / community / movement — the actual brand.
05Tell me a winner  We'll bring it up to a full storefront with real product detail pages, cart, and the rest of the page tree. We can also remix two — borrow Casa's crew wall into Tropical's itinerary, or Signal's drop into Halftime, etc.
