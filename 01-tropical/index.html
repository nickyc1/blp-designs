Baby Let's Padel is a Houston padel community built by two Latinas — apparel, open plays, and a movement on the court. Mixed levels. All genders. Bring a friend, bring water, traemos el resto.

We started Baby Let's Padel out of a group chat that wouldn't quit and a couple of Houston courts that needed regulars. Now we host open plays four nights a week and travel for tournaments. The kit funds the courts. The courts feed the crew.
