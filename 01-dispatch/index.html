Editor's Letter Issue 01
Sec. A — p. 03
by Baby Let's Padel

We started this because nobody else was going to.

Padel arrived in Houston the way it always does — a couple of courts, a few obsessed regulars, a group chat that moved too fast to read. What was missing was the kit. Real training apparel for the players showing up week after week, not a pastel capsule for the off-court photo.

So we made it. Heavy crewnecks for the cold mornings. A lightweight shell for August in Texas. A six-panel cap that holds its shape after fifty wears. The brand is the people who play.

This is Issue 01 — the spring dispatch. Inside: open play schedule through May, a feature on our Osaka collab, the spring kit, and a few notes on where we'll be next.
