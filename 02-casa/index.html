building padel
in Houston.~ Houston, TX ~

Baby Let's Padel started as a group chat — two Latinas in Houston who couldn't find a regular padel night that felt like home. So we made one. The kit funds the courts. The courts feed the crew. The crew is the brand.

Co-founder~ The captain Runs the schedule, the group chat, and a mean third-shot lob. Houston-raised. Insists everyone learns each other's name before warmup ends.
Co-founder~ The builder Designs the kit and books the courts. Came to padel from tennis and never went back. Still beats most of the regulars on a slow Tuesday.
