— The Field Log

Notes from the road.

What we packed, what we played, what we learned. Each entry is a pin on the atlas. Updated after every trip.

Entry 008 · 04 / 18

The case for a second visor.

Houston in April is humid before noon. We packed one visor each on the Cube Padel scout trip and rotated through three. Lesson: bring two. Wash one. Wear the other.

RSC KatyBy BLP
Entry 009 · 04 / 21

Why the navy crewneck outsells.

The deep navy heavy crew has been the most-worn piece across our last six open plays. Theory: it's the only one that hides court dust between sets and still photographs well after.

Inventory noteBy BLP
Entry 010 · 04 / 26

Osaka Vision Pro vs. Vision Tour.

14 sessions, two frames. Vision Pro for aggressive baseliners, Vision Tour for control players. Both run light enough for a long open play. Demos courtside every Tuesday.
