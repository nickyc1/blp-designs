Two Latinas, one studio,
desde Houston.~ poco a poco ~

The whole company is run out of a Houston studio by two Latinas — designing the kit, hosting the open plays, and answering the DMs themselves. Every piece is made by people who have to play in it the next morning.

Co-founder~ Diseño + atelier Designs every piece in the kit. Came to padel from tennis, never went back. Won't ship a piece she hasn't worn through three open plays first.
Co-founder~ Comunidad + open plays Runs the schedule, the courts, and the group chat. Houston-raised. Insists everyone learns each other's name before warmup ends.
Plate 02

From the atelier.

Three principles · slow build
01

Made for the player on the court, not the photo.

We design what we wear when nobody is watching. Cotton that survives a Tuesday-night session. Knits that move with the swing. Caps that hold their shape after fifty wears.

02

Sized for movement, not for a model.

Unisex sizing across the whole kit. We patterned it on the people we play with — five-foot-two to six-foot-four, all body types, all genders. If it doesn't fit, send it back.

03

Slow drops, not seasonal hype.

Two issues a year — spring and fall. Six pieces each. Restocks announced in the newsletter. We'd rather under-make than overproduce.
