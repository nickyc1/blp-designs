05 / Operating Principles

House rules for the courts we play on.

Baby Let's Padel is community-first. The kit funds the courts. The courts feed the crew. Every open play we host runs on the same playbook, regardless of city.

Mixed levels. Rotating doubles. Rackets and balls on us. New players welcome — we'll find you a fourth before warmup ends.

R.01Show up. Hit balls. Stay for the second game.
R.02Mixed levels welcome. We will find you a fourth.
R.03The crew is the brand. The kit is the by-product.
R.04Houston is home base. Everywhere else is a road game.
R.05Bring a friend who's never played. We'll teach them.
R.06Argue line calls less. Take the next point harder.
R.07Demo Osaka rackets courtside. Tuesdays at RSC Katy.
